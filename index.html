About me

After watching the documentary "Girls Can Code", I came to the realisation that I was in the wrong job and a future in development was the way forward!
I tried teaching myself for a bit and then I signed up for a coding bootcamp - the Web Development Immersive (WDI) at General Assembly). It was a pretty intense 12 week programme where we used a range of technologies such as JavaScript, AngularJS, Ruby on Rails, Node.js, Express, HTML, CSS, SASS and so much more.
It's been an awesome ride so far, I've learnt soooo much, and I'm excited for what's coming next!
When I'm not coding, you can find me on the dancefloor lindyhopping, portrait sketching or sewing up a storm!
