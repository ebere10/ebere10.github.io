BACK TO THE FUTURE

Seventies style returns in a big way for a/w 2018, and the new collection from Hush has everything you need to wear the trend

Folk-inspired florals, animal prints and velvet as far as the eye can see… Yes, Seventies style is very much having a moment, and the a/w collection from Hush has drawn heavily on one of fashion’s boldest decades.

Seventies silhouettes combine with tactile, autumn-friendly fabrics to create a collection that’s as cosily wearable as it is on-trend. Think green velvet blazers, slouchy boots and rolled-up corduroy trousers, alongside fully-fledged showstoppers like a faux-fur statement coat in leopard print. And with party season approaching, we’ve also got an eye on a selection of floaty midi dresses, available in a host of winter florals.

Fire up the disco ball and fall back in love with all things bright and bold. The Seventies is back and it’s coming for your wardrobe…
